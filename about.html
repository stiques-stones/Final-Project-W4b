About us


Stiques & Stones was established and created in 2016 during the beginning of the pandemic where there was alot of uncertainty surrounding our normal way of life. Stephanie, the owner of Stiques & Stones, always thrived to be a part of the creative world on her own terms. Stiques & Stones started off sellling one of kind, curated vintage. Eventually progressing to upcycled vintage, Stephanie started recreating garments from damaged, vintage styles. Eventually, creating her own patterns and creating her own originally designed collection in May of 2017. We now specialize in originally designed garments created from vintage fabric and reworked garments.
